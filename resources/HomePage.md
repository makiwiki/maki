## Welcome to your Maki wiki

### Usage

* How can I create & edit pages?
  * Just create & edit markdown files in **/Apps/Maki** folder of your Dropbox.

* How can I put images?
  * Just place image files in **/Apps/Maki** folder of your Dropbox.
